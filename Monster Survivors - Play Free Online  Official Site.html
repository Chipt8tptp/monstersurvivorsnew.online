About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive action roguelike survival game where you face off against hordes of relentless monsters. With each wave, the enemies grow stronger and more numerous, testing your skills and strategy to the limit.

Choose your character, collect experience points by defeating monsters, and unlock powerful upgrades and abilities. Every run is unique, with random item drops and skill combinations that allow for endless replayability.

Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick game or a hardcore gamer aiming for the highest score, Monster Survivors offers hours of exciting gameplay for everyone.

How to Play

Controls

  • • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • • Mouse: Aim your attacks
  • • Left Click: Fire primary weapon
  • • Space: Use special ability

Tips for Survival

  • • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • • Prioritize upgrades that match your playstyle
  • • Collect health pickups to stay alive
  • • Combine different skills for maximum effect
